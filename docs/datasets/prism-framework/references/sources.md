## Key Clinical Evidence

### Metabolic / Insulin Resistance
- UKPDS — Metformin outcomes in T2DM
- Bannister 2014 — Metformin-treated diabetics vs non-diabetic controls (matched cohort, lifespan)
- TAME Trial — Targeting Aging with Metformin (Barzilai, design published, results pending)
- Konopka 2019 — Metformin blunts exercise-induced mitochondrial adaptation

### Cardiovascular
- FOURIER — Evolocumab, aggressive LDL/ApoB lowering
- ODYSSEY — Alirocumab outcomes
- JUPITER — Rosuvastatin primary prevention (hsCRP-guided)
- SELECT — Semaglutide CVD outcomes (17% MACE reduction)
- MESA — CAC score and risk prediction
- SCOT-HEART — CCTA utility
- ASPREE 2018 — Aspirin in healthy elderly (increased mortality)
- USPSTF 2022 — Updated aspirin primary prevention recommendation
- Sniderman, EAS consensus — ApoB as superior risk marker
- ONTARGET — Telmisartan outcomes

### Longevity / Geroprotection
- NIA Interventions Testing Program (ITP):
  - Harrison 2009 — Rapamycin lifespan extension
  - Harrison 2014 — Acarbose lifespan extension
  - Strong 2016 — 17α-estradiol male lifespan extension
  - ITP 2019 — Canagliflozin male lifespan extension
  - Nature Aging 2025 — Rapamycin + trametinib additive effects
  - Gehan re-analysis 2024 — Enalapril, metformin, caffeic acid effects
- Mannick 2014/2018 — Rapamycin analogs and immune aging (human)
- PEARL Trial — Rapamycin human aging (emerging)

### Senolytics
- Kirkland 2017 — Dasatinib + Quercetin mouse healthspan
- Justice 2019 — D+Q human pilot (idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis)
- AFFIRM-LITE — Fisetin trial
